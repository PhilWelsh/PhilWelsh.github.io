Early Ambitions

For as far back as i can remember i have been interested in creative arts, and following art and creative media led me to Teesside University where I studied Creative Digital Media. Here I learned a lot about various medias, graphic design art and animation.

Gap years

After finishing my degree, i decided to take a year out to decide where my future would be - I became a foreign teacher in China for the original intention of staying just one year. I ended up staying almost 3. i loved it, exploring nearby countries and learning the language and culture which i was quite unaware of.

Career Path

Having time to 'laterally drift' helped me make some decisions, i talked among other like-minded people and considered careers where i could use my enthusiasm, personability, curiosity and creativity to good use.

ne-web

Fighting my lack of paper experience i gained a job as a web designer at a small web-design agency, where i would still be able to use HTML CSS and Javascript but where the lions share of my time would be used for design-work amongst other things such as photography/imagery, stationary-design, pitching to clients and copywriting.

Being part of a small 4-man team i was able to learn a lot about business and working with clients.

Lowes

Invited to work at a small IFA in jesmond as part of a small software and web development team creating in house products. I worked closely with professionals with up to 10 years experience in the programming languages and methodologies i was very interested in learning.

Next?

This has whet my appetite for further learning and i am eager to be part of larger tech teams and work on more advanced projects where i could really get involved with higher level code
